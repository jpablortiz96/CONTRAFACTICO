# CONTRAFÁCTICO 2:30 Demo Script

## 0–15s — The Problem

**Visual:** Open on the CONTRAFÁCTICO hero.

**Narration:**

“Bad decisions rarely fail because people are careless. They fail because a premise was already false, a warning existed, and the right people never saw it. CONTRAFÁCTICO finds the branch where the better future split away.”

## 15–40s — Microsoft 365 Copilot Rewind

**Visual:** In Microsoft 365 Copilot, Teams, or Copilot Studio, ask: `Rewind our decision to launch the X-200 in March.`

**Narration:**

“The agent calls a Copilot-safe MCP facade running on Azure Container Apps. Foundry IQ grounds the answer in the company evidence base. It finds a February 14 supplier warning that contradicted `supplier_on_time`. The warning was intended for four decision makers and read by zero.”

## 40–75s — Web War Room and the $80K Gap

**Visual:** Open **Rewind Demo**, select **Run Rewind**, and follow the glowing counterfactual branch.

**Narration:**

“The War Room reconstructs the observed path and the evidence-supported alternative. The March launch led to stockout pressure and $80,000 in Q1 returns. Acting on the warning moves launch to April and avoids the documented return cost. Unsupported claims are dropped instead of presented as fact.”

## 75–105s — Enterprise Cockpit and the $142K Pattern

**Visual:** Open **Enterprise Cockpit**. Show the KPI row, onboarding wizard, and connector wall.

**Narration:**

“Across four synthetic decisions, CONTRAFÁCTICO identifies $142,000 in avoidable exposure, one live fork, three governance blocks, and 92 percent average branch reliability. The cockpit also shows exactly how evidence enters, how decisions are normalized, and which connectors are implemented, contracted, documented, or still pending.”

## 105–130s — Evidence Graph and Governance

**Visual:** Select the February 14 warning and March 31 finance nodes in the Evidence Graph, then show the governance policy panel.

**Narration:**

“The graph makes every relationship inspectable: the launch rests on a premise, the warning contradicts it, decision makers missed it, and finance prices the outcome. A deterministic policy blocks high-impact recommendations when contradicted evidence has low readership. The agent requires human approval and never acts automatically.”

## 130–150s — Adoption and Closing

**Visual:** Show the channel matrix and production topology.

**Narration:**

“A customer replaces the synthetic corpus with approved company evidence, builds a Decision Registry, grounds it through Foundry IQ, and delivers cited analysis through Microsoft 365 Copilot, Teams, the Web War Room, MCP, Power Automate, Power BI, or internal portals. CONTRAFÁCTICO: see the decision your company should have made — and what it would have saved.”
